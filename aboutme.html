HELLO

Always curious & open-minded about what is surrounding me, my creativity comes from my travels, discoveries, and with experiences that I acquired during my studies at ISD in France & in India.
Since my childhood, I have always been manual by creating games. Now, expressing myself through prototypes and doodles is for me a simple and effective way. Sport also represents a strong family value. Playing volleyball since 15years learned me to be involve and have a the team spirit.
My different journeys brought to me a versatile design process. My high school degree in engineering sciences helps me to make analyst and achievable projects. I learned the functional and aesthetic aspect of the product thanks to ISD school. My last internship at Studio Banana bring me the sense of details and the storytelling of the product.

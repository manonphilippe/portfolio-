Industrial Design

A children's motor skills course for Domyos

I was in charge of creating the first Decathlon _motricity course_. A product that can grow with the child. The main objective was to analyse the different stages of a child's motricity from 0 to 6 years old in order to map its evolution and propose relevant products.

Details

  • Client: Decathlon - Domyos Gym Pilates
  • My role: Industrial Designer Intern
  • Timeline: Sept. - Dec. 2017 (3 months)
  • Tools used: Interviews with a psychomotricist, Making mock-up scale 1:1, Adobe Illustrator, 3D rhino and Keyshot

Context

In order to create a range of motor accessories for children, Domyos has set up a team consisting of a product manager, an engineer and a designer to develop new products. Beginning their range with carpets, balls, hoops and tunnels their aim was to create a _motor skills courses_ according to the age of the child and the constraints of home interiors.

Opportunity

I was in charge of creating a motor skills course for children aged 0 to 6 years old that can be used at home. Being an intern designer during this project, I was not able to finish the development of the product. My work consisted of the analysis of the needs, the motor skills of the child, the concept-product proposals and the phase 1 of the development of the final product.
